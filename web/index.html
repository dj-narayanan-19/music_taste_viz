The map

Every song is a bubble on the map positioned based on its sound qualities. Songs that sound alike are put near each other.

Bubble size

Bigger = more plays.

Color modes

Artist — 50 artists with the most plays in the dataset are colored.
Neighborhood — groups all songs into 6 broad sonic clusters. Click a cluster to see what connects them.

Feature bars

Hover any song to see its audio fingerprint — 8 bars for acousticness, danceability, energy, and more. The faint tick marks the global average for each feature. What do these mean?

Sonic neighbors

Click any song to highlight its 15 most sonically similar neighbors. Click it again to clear.
